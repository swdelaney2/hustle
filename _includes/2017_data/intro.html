The heavens opened up on our 8th annual Hustle Up The Hill. In fact, it was one of the rainiest days in Chicago this year. Due to the heavy rains, we had to make the difficult decision to call the race. Yet our wonderful participants, generous donors and dedicated sponsors made this one of the most successful Hustle events to date.

Through your generosity, we raised enough money to support a Bridge family for 18 months, more than a year and a half of support for a family in need. Our work for ending homelessness is not done, but your participation will help us continue this important work.

Family #8 recently graduated from the program. She shared with us the following message:

"Thank you so much to everyone who has supported me in any type of way during my time in Bridge. I wouldn't be where I am without the support from you all. . . Because of my mentors and all of you, I will be able to have a future of stability for my daughter."

We wish her and her daughter a very bright future. We are pleased to announce that we have just welcomed Family #10 to the program.
